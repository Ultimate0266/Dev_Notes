Nakamura Hits 50th Bullet Title
FIDE Rating Lists Shake Up for 2026
by Saefullah Sadiq

Hikaru Nakamura was crowned as Bullet Brawl's first "half-centurion" on January 3, 2026, after winning his 50th title in the event's first edition of the year.

The first official rating list of 2026 shows Magnus Carlsen maintaining a firm lead as world number one, followed by Hikaru Nakamura and Fabiano Caruana.
